# Entity: KickstartAI

**Last updated:** 2026-06-16

## Summary
KickstartAI is an independent non-profit foundation on a mission to accelerate AI adoption in the Dutch ecosystem, so that "everyone should benefit from AI." It runs multiple applied AI projects simultaneously and openly shares its learnings with the wider Dutch ecosystem.

## Details
- **Type:** Independent non-profit foundation
- **Mission:** Accelerate AI adoption in the Dutch ecosystem; bridge the "impact gap" by moving AI from isolated experiments to broad national implementation.
- **History:** Founded in 2019 by four of the largest Dutch companies to accelerate AI adoption in the Netherlands; became an independent non-profit in 2022 while maintaining close ties to its founding partners.
- **Founding partners:** Ahold Delhaize, ING, KLM, NS
- **Community:** A network of ~7,500 AI professionals.

### Three pillars (how they work)
1. **We do** — Partner with businesses, governments, and societal organizations to develop practical AI solutions and best practices, moving projects from isolated experiments to broad implementation.
2. **We share** — Openly share learnings and knowledge with the entire Dutch ecosystem. This pillar directly underpins the knowledge-sharing / blog rationale of the Living Wiki's [[generator-module]] ("replicable patterns" shared with the ecosystem).
3. **We connect** — Act as bridge-builders, bringing together leaders, policymakers, and the community of AI professionals.

### Role in this project
Originator of the Living Wiki brief; provides a technical mentor (TBD) and access to its engineering team. The Living Wiki architecture is intended for reuse across all KickstartAI projects.
