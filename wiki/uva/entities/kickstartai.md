# Entity: KickstartAI

**Last updated:** 2026-06-16

## Summary
KickstartAI is a non-profit foundation on a mission to accelerate AI adoption in the Dutch ecosystem. It runs multiple applied AI projects simultaneously.

## Details
- **Type:** Non-profit foundation
- **Mission:** Accelerate AI adoption in the Dutch ecosystem
- **Partners:** KLM, ING, Ahold Delhaize, NS
- **Role in this project:** Originator of the Living Wiki brief; provides technical mentor and access to its engineering team. The Living Wiki architecture is intended for reuse across all KickstartAI projects.
