## Summary
The **GenAI-Analytic Problem Structure (GAPS) diagram** is the problem-definition framework used in the team’s Assignment 1a report (see [[assignment-1-report-2026-04-22]]) to structure the Living Wiki’s problem definition. It is the **GenAI-adapted form of the DAPS-diagram method** of **de Mast & Lokkerbol (2024), “DAPS diagrams for defining Data Science projects,” *Journal of Big Data* 11(1)**.
