# Deliverable: Wiki Generation Engine

**Last updated:** 2026-06-16
**Status:** scoped

## Summary
An LLM-powered system that processes ingested documents into structured, interlinked wiki pages organized by topic. Pages regenerate or update on a configurable schedule (e.g., daily) as source material changes.
