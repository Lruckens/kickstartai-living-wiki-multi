# AVG / DPIA

**Concept:** Privacy and compliance

## Summary
An AVG (GDPR) compliance assessment and a DPIA are required **before** live launch. The FG, [[marja-lievens]], will be involved from the start. Interaction logging for quality monitoring must be AVG-compliant.

## Open considerations
- Which "act" capabilities (writing actions on behalf of citizens) are feasible within AVG/DPIA constraints is not yet clear. See [[_gaps]].
