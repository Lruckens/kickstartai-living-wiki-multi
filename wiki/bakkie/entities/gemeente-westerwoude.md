# Gemeente Westerwoude

**Type:** Organisation (client municipality)

## Summary
Dutch municipality of ~140,000 inhabitants, comprising three urban districts plus seven surrounding kernen. Client for Project Bakkie. The college has committed to digital-first service delivery in the 2024–2028 coalition agreement, with the waste domain as the starting point.

## Details
- Handles ~38,000 citizen contacts/year on waste-collection topics — roughly twice the per-capita load of comparable municipalities, attributed to a complex split-collection system (rest, GFT, PMD, papier, plus milieustraten for grofvuil).
- Contact channels: ~62% phone (KCC), ~24% webformulier, remainder email/loket.
- Waste was chosen as the first domain due to volume and citizen-frustration scores in the 2024 burgerpeiling.
