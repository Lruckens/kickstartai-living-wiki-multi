## Bridging the impact gap

We believe that AI can help build a brighter future for all. But all too often, much of its potential remains locked away in short-term fixes and isolated experiments. We're here to bridge that gap and move from concentrated actions to national impact.

## Our founding partners

In 2019, four of the largest Dutch companies came together to discuss the future of AI. They recognized its potential to improve lives. They also agreed that the whole country would only benefit from a joint effort. KickstartAI was launched to accelerate the adoption of AI in the Netherlands.

In 2022, we became an independent non-profit, but we maintain close relationships with our founding partners Ahold Delhaize, ING, KLM, and NS. We drive the initiatives that strengthen the Dutch AI ecosystem and create a sustainable positive impact.
