## Tackling real issues

We're doers by nature, using AI to tackle real-world challenges. Together with our partners, we collaborate on practical use cases with their in-house teams. Our goal? To help organizations move from endless pilots and isolated experiments to the implementation of working AI solutions at scale.

## Apply

We partner with organizations to develop AI solutions that solve pressing challenges. We move past the pilot phase to focus on embedding AI into practice. This is how we create proof points, best practices, and lessons everyone can benefit from.

## Amplify

We openly share our learnings with the entire Dutch ecosystem so that everyone can build on our work. We also foster a vibrant community of 7,500+ AI professionals centered around connection, knowledge exchange, and co-creation.

## Activate

We bring together business leaders and policymakers to turn AI capabilities into a joint national advantage through direct action and knowledge exchange.
